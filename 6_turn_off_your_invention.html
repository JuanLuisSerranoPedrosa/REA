6. Turn off your invention

Dictionary

Achieve

Definition:

Be successful doing what you planned.

Ser exitoso haciendo lo que planeaste.

Example:

I have achieved this task.

He logrado esta tarea.

Spanish word:

Lograr

Audio:

Summary

Definition:

The most important information of something.

La información más importante de algo.

Example:

I am reading the summary of this book.

Estoy leyendo el resumen de este libro.

Spanish word:

Resumen

Audio:

Turn off

Definition:

To stop something by pressing a button.

Parar algo presionando un botón.

Example:

Turn off the radio, please.

Apaga la radio, por favor.

Spanish word:

Apagar
