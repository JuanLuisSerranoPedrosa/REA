4. The invention is getting closer

Dictionary

Cheap

Definition:

Something costing less than usual.

Algo que cuesta menos de lo normal.

Example:

That car is cheap, you don't need a lot of money to buy it.

Ese coche es barato, tú no necesitas mucho dinero para comprarlo.

Spanish word:

Barato

Audio:

Expensive

Definition:

Costing a lot of money.

Algo que cuesta mucho dinero.

Example:

That car is expensive you need a lot of money to buy it.

Ese coche es caro, necesitas mucho dinero para comprarlo.

Spanish word:

Caro

Audio:

Fair

Definition:

An event to show some products or services.

Un evento para mostrar algunos productos o servicios.

Example:

There is an invention fair at the school.

Hay una feria de inventos en el colegio.

Spanish word:

Feria

Audio:

Heavy

Definition:

Something that weighs a lot.

Algo que pesa mucho.

Example:

A ship is heavy.

Un barco es pesado.

Spanish word:

Pesado

Audio:

Iron

Definition:

A dark grey metal.

Metal oscuro y gris.

Example:

A car is made of iron.

Un coche está hecho de hierro.

Spanish word:

Hierro

Audio:

Lapbook

Definition:

Cardboard book used to present information about a topic.

Libro de cartulina que se utiliza para presentar la información acerca de un tema.

Example:

We made a Science lapbook.

Nosotros hicimos un lapbook de ciencias.

Spanish word:

Lapbook

Audio:

Shape

Definition:

The physical form of something.

La forma física de algo.

Example:

I cut circular shapes.

Yo corté formas circulares.

Spanish word:

Formas
