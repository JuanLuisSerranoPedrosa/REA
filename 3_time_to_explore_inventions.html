3. Time to explore inventions!

Dictionary

Advertisement

Definition:

A picture, short film, etc. that is designed to persuade people to buying something.

Una fotografía, vídeo corto, etc. diseñado para convencer a la gente de que compre algo.

Example:

I watched an advertisement of a new car.

Yo vi un anuncio de un coche nuevo.

Spanish word:

Anuncio

Audio:

Delivery

Definition:

The act of taking things to a person or place.

Acción de llevar cosas a personas o lugares.

Example:

A truck makes hundreds of deliveries everyday.

Un camión realizar cientos de repartos al día.

Spanish word:

Reparto

Audio:

Sales

Definition:

A product reduced in price.

Producto con su precio reducido.

Example:

The shops usually have sales after Christmas.

Las tiendas normalmente tienen ofertas después de las Navidades.

Spanish word:

Rebajas

Audio:

Skateboard

Definition:

A board with wheels on the bottom that you stand on and move forward by pushing one foot on the ground.

Tabla con ruedas en la parte inferior sobre la que te pones de pie y te mueves hacia delante empujando con un pie sobre el suelo.

Example:

She uses her skateboard everyday.

Ella utiliza su patinete todos los días.

Spanish word:

Patinete

4. Will I be able to do the activity?

You have already started the activity "Unity is strength"! Beginnings can create fears and insecurities.

If you complete STEP 1 of the Learning Journal (Will I be able to do it?) you will see that your feelings are normal when we start a task. To do this, you must organize your ideas through strategies that help you remember the information.

Remember:

  • Fill it out on your computer, following the instructions in your Clasroom. Don't forget to send it when you're done!

¿Necesitas más ayuda?

¡Ya has empezado la actividad  "La unión hace la fuerza"! Los comienzos pueden crearte miedos e inseguridades.
